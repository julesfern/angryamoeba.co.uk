My name is Dan Glegg. I'm a software geek based in Brighton, UK.

I'm interested in building awesome products, teams and businesses around new and interesting technologies. I mainly enjoy working with Ruby, Objective-C/Cocoa, Flex, and XHTML/CSS/Javascript. From time to time I speak about these things at public events. I'm also a passionate advocate for open standards, open software and open licensing.
