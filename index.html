We love making things happen with software. We love it enough that we build our own products, create our own startup companies and invest in existing startups who need technical assistance to realise their products. We love building and looking after our own products, and we love working with startup companies. Startup companies are awesome because they have the newest and most interesting ideas - but the really ambitious ones need just a little more technical oomph to get off the ground. That's were we come in. Here at Angry amoeba we do only three things. We build our own products, create our own startup companies and invest in the development of products for existing startup companies who need a little more technical oomph to get their ideas off the ground.
